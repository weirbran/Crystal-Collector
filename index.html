Crystal Collector

How to Play:

A random number is displayed below. When you click on one of the crystals, it adds a random, specific amount of points to your total score. You win if your total score matches the random number displayed by the computer. You lose if your score goes above the random number.

The game restarts whenever the you win or lose. When the game restarts, you'll see a new random number and your total score will be reset to zero. Also, all the crystals will have four new values. The score counter will keep track of your wins and losses. Do not refresh the page as a means to restart the game.
